Momos

Momos are a type of steamed dumpling originating from Tibet and Nepal, and are also popular in Bhutan and parts of India. They are typically filled with savory ingredients, such as vegetables, meat, or cheese, and are often served with a dipping sauce.

Making of Momos

  1. Make the dough
  2. Prepare the filling
  3. Assemble the momos
  4. Steam the momos
  5. Serve

Tips

  • You can find pre-made momo wrappers in most Asian grocery stores.
  • Feel free to experiment with different vegetables and spices in the filling.
  • Don't overcook the momos, otherwise they will become tough.
